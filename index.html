Conspiracy

One day you wake up with a future book that shows you important events for 1 year- in the future. Suddenly, you're being chased by a psychopathic man intent on killing you to get to your book and a group of people with similar books who want you to join them so you can use the future books to your advantage. Every choice you make affects the outcome of the story. Will you survive the experience or die?
