Conspiracy

There's something about this group that seems special- either that, or you're still afraid to face down any killers again. Either way, you don't want to be alone right now. In only a few weeks, you're completely integrated into the group, and you enjoy the company of your new friends. Together, you look through the future books and make plans for the next twenty years. You all know how helpful these books will be to become rich and successful, and doesn't everyone want that?

Over the next couple years, you finish a few future books, and use the information you got from them to make the best choices in supporting companies and stocks. Your 'amazing' skills at finding high-potential companies is renowned in many places, and soon, you and your group members have all become billionaires. After twenty years passes, you still have more than enough to live a life of luxury.

You thank your younger self for choosing to stay with this group, since it's gotten you nothing but happiness and success.

YOU SURVIVED!
