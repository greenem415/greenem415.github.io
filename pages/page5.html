Conspiracy

You tell the girl you don't want to join her group, you just want to go back to your normal life. You offer up yours and the dead man's future books to 'A'. Instead of taking them, she just smiles apologetically. 'I see,' she says, 'I'm afraid I can't let you go with the knowledge of these books then.' She jerks forward, pulls out a gun from her jacket, and expertly shoots you in the heart. You gasp in pain and stumble back, clutching the bloody hole in your chest. 'A' picks up the books you dropped and walks away.
